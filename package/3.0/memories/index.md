## Memories

Memories are Reitti's innovative way of transforming your raw location data into meaningful, narrative-driven travel logs. Instead of sifting through endless GPS points, Memories help you curate and relive your journeys in a structured, visual format.

### What Are Memories?
Memories are generated from your existing location data, serving as the foundation for each entry. Once created, they are automatically enhanced with relevant images and text descriptions pulled from your tracked data. This process turns scattered geopoints into cohesive stories, making it easier to reflect on past trips, adventures, or daily routines.

### Creating Memories
You can create a new Memory at any time using your historical data as a base. The creation process involves selecting a time range and other details, after which Reitti analyzes your geopoints to build the Memory. Note that this may take some time, especially if there are many images or data points to process.

### Editing and Sharing
Once a Memory is created, you have full control to edit and modify it—such as adding notes, adjusting dates, or refining the content. Sharing is seamless: use MagicLinks to share read-only versions with others, or collaborate in real-time by sharing an editable link. This allows friends, family, or travel partners to contribute, making Memories a social tool for group storytelling.
